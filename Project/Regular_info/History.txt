*                  Bangladesh University of Business and Technology (BUBT)                        *
***************************************************************************************************

BUBT was established under the Private University Act in the year 2003, following the model of 
North American Universities. It received approval from the University Grants Commission (UGC) and 
the Ministry of Education, Government of Bangladesh. BUBT was founded by Dhaka Commerce College, 
Mirpur-2, Dhaka, and is managed by a Board of Trustees and a Syndicate.

BUBT is a leading private sector university in Bangladesh, known for providing quality education 
and training since its inception. Today, BUBT has established itself as one of the top-ranking 
private universities in the country. This recognition has been achieved by meeting all the 
requirements of the Private University Act, 2010, and maintaining high standards in education, 
research facilities, a transparent examination system, updated academic programs, essential physical
 infrastructure, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities, modern student facilities, and a 
disciplined environment.
